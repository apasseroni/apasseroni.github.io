About

Experience

Over the course of my professional journey, I've had the privilege to work on cutting-edge projects in defense and immerse myself in the dynamic realm of independent game development. From engineering software for advanced naval systems at Raytheon Technologies to founding and navigating the challenges of my own game studio, GLHF Studios, each experience has shaped my expertise and fueled my passion for creating impactful software solutions. Here's a glimpse into my professional milestones:

Raytheon Technologies

At Raytheon Technologies, I played a pivotal role as a Senior Software Engineer on the U.S. Navy Zumwalt class destroyer program. I designed, developed, tested, and maintained software for human-computer interaction and external communications. My excellent performance in this role was recognized with seven achievement awards.

GLHF Studios

In 2022, I founded GLHF Studios, stepping into the world of independent game development. As the sole developer, I've been navigating the challenges and joys of wearing all of the different hats required to make engaging interactive experiences. This endeavor has pushed me to grow technically, creatively, and personally.

Asteroid Miner

Developed a small game for a week-long game jam. The goal of the jam was to reimagine the classic Atari arcade game, "Asteroids", but with a twist. I chose to change the game by adding a mining mechanic, so in addition to dodging and destroying asteroids you also have to deploy astronauts to mine valuable resources from them. Astronauts only have a limited supply of oxygen though, so be sure to pick them up before time runs out!

Threes Clone

This was a purely educational project that I made early on in my transition to game development. I had made a lot of prototypes in Unity but wanted to try my hand at making a full game. In order to remove any non-technical roadblocks from the process, I chose to recreate the popular mobile game, "Threes", which is a game that I've always admired for it's simple and effective design.

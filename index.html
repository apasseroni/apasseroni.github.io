About Me

I'm a software engineer from Rhode Island with 9 years of combined experience in the fields of defense and gaming. Regardless of the project, I'm passionate about crafting applications with a focus on the user's experience and my unique blend of coding and design expertise enables me to do that effectively. I'm currently self-employed as I work on releasing the first game under my personal company, GLHF Studios.

My Experience

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to develop software for cutting-edge Naval warships and craft fun and engaging games. Here's a summary of what I've done thus far:

Raytheon Technologies

As a Senior Software Engineer on the U.S. Navy Zumwalt class destroyer program, I played a pivotal role designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software for human-computer interaction and external communications.

GLHF Studios

In 2022 I started GLHF Studios, stepping into the world of independent game development. As a solo developer, I've been navigating the challenges and joys of wearing all of the different hats required to make engaging interactive experiences.

Asteroid Miner

I developed this arcade style game for a week-long game jam where the goal was to recreate the classic Atari arcade game, "Asteroids" with a twist. I chose to add a mining mechanic where the player has to deploy astronauts to mine valuable resources from asteroids.

Threes Clone

This was an educational project that I made to go through the process of making a complete game. In order to remove any non-technical roadblocks, I chose to recreate the popular mobile game "Threes" which is a game that I've always admired for its minimalist design.
